When anyone dev on Windows or use the console in any other way, you need reliable environment wich will help you to do your job faster.

ConEmu is a portable, handy, smart, reliable and fast terminal, full of features which are traditionally available to MacOS and Unix users.

ConEmu gives you full control over console. User may do almost anything via keyboard, mouse, touchscreen or GuiMacro. For example: resizing window, quake-style activation, copying and pasting text, tabs and splits navigation, scrolling console, fast access to your favourite shells and much more...

Large amount of options let you set up the terminal of your dream.
