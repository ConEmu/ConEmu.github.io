ConEmu

эмулятор терминала

ConEmu may "pause" your ConsoleApplication output. Just press Win+A or press the "lock" button on the ToolBar.

ConEmu will load the entire console back-scroll buffer in memory (that may takes few seconds in some cases) and display it. If any ConsoleApplication will change output in the RealConsole at that time, changes will not be displayed in ConEmu until you exit AlternativeBuffer by pressing Esc or Win+A.
